Tier-2 & Tier-3 hospitals · HMS Platform

The problem

Tier-2 and Tier-3 hospitals run on disconnected systems — and patients bear the cost.

Registration, OPD, lab, pharmacy, billing — each running on separate software or paper. Duplicate patient records, billing errors, and lost prescriptions are daily occurrences. Staff spend more time managing data than caring for patients.

Multiple patient IDs across departments — no single source of truth
Manual billing consolidation across lab, pharmacy, and consultation
Paper prescriptions and reports — slow, error-prone, hard to audit
No DR or backup on hospital data — operational risk to patient records

Our solution

HMS — a workflow-first hospital platform built on secure hybrid cloud.

A connected Hospital Management System that links every department through a single UHID-led patient journey. Registration to follow-up in one operating flow — built for hospitals that need reliability, not complexity.

The problem

Manufacturing operations are increasingly data-heavy — but infrastructure rarely keeps pace.

Multi-site operations, manual provisioning, and legacy infrastructure create DR exposure, compliance gaps, and rising operating costs. When production systems go down, every hour matters.

Compliance gaps across backup, data retention, and audit logging

An operations platform designed for the data demands of modern manufacturing.

Compliance-ready platforms · Government workflow digitisation

The problem

Public sector organisations face tightening compliance requirements on infrastructure that was never designed for them.

India's DPDP Act and rising cyber threats put data-heavy government operations under new scrutiny. Legacy infrastructure without proper backup, encryption, or audit logging creates both compliance and operational risk.

DPDP compliance gaps — data residency, retention, and access control requirements unmet
Legacy infrastructure with no documented DR or tested recovery procedures
Paper-based workflows creating data silos and audit gaps

Our approach

Compliance-ready platforms built on secure, auditable hybrid cloud infrastructure.

We apply the same secure platform engineering we deliver to enterprises — with the data sovereignty, audit logging, and access control requirements that public sector contexts demand.

Planned capabilities

DPDP-ready data infrastructure with audit trails
On-prem hybrid cloud for data sovereignty requirements
Workflow digitisation on secure cloud foundations
Ransomware-resilient DR for critical public data
