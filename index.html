Client Outcomes

From fragmented backup tools to a unified immutable strategy

Problem: DR tests were inconsistent across virtual machines and Kubernetes clusters, with uncertain recovery windows.

What we changed: Designed a unified Veeam + Kasten protection architecture with immutable backup tiers, policy-driven restores, and quarterly DR runbooks.

Problem: The infra team spent too much time on repetitive cluster operations and patch windows were high-risk.

What we changed: Introduced infrastructure automation pipelines, hardened OpenShift baseline controls, and role-based operational guardrails.

Outcome: Manual platform ops reduced by 31% with predictable maintenance windows and faster issue triage.
